CADT Student Association Team Building Trip

24th October 2022

CADT Student Association recently organized a team building trip for the student body to the Garden City Water Park. The trip provided a much-needed break for the students of all generations. The trip was planned by the Student Association and was open to all students of CADT. The students were able to enjoy a day of fun and relaxation at the water park. Students were able to take part in a variety of activities such as swimming, water slides, and other water-based activities. They also had the opportunity to bond with one another and build new relationships with their senior/ junior.

CADT Student Association also organized a scavenger hunt-like game where the students formed a team of two and have complete tasks assign to them. The idea behind this was to help the students bond and get to know each other better.

The students were also given the chance to explore the park and its attractions. They were able to go on the water slides, wave pools, lazy river, and more. Garden City Water Park also has a big variety of food stalls ranging from traditional food to western food. Students were satisfied with the food and drinks they had. CADT Student Association was pleased with the outcome of the trip and will be looking to organize more team building trips in the future.
