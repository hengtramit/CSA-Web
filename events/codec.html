CODE-C, THE FIRST DEVELOPER CONFERENCE IN CAMBODIA

20th June 2022

CODE-C is a developer conference and technology exhibition for programmers, tech entrepreneurs and digital talents to support digital innovation made in Cambodia and build the digital future of Cambodia. CODE-C aims to bring together participants from the technology ecosystem, digital companies, government and educational institutions. The event had panels and keynotes that are joined by the industry's leading speakers and key innovators, attendees was able to learn about the latest trends and key challenges, from infrastructure to systems and processes.

The majority of CADT Student Association members helped in organizing the event. It was well-attended and enjoyed by all who participated. Members across all generation each volunteered on taking a responsiblity and a role in order to ensure that the necessary preparation are met before the event and they also worked hard to make sure that the event would run smoothly. All the volunteers also cleaned up after the event, making sure that the campus ground, which was where the 2-day "CODE-C" event was held, left in a better state than it was before the event.


CODE-C was a huge success, with over 2500 participants from all over Phnom Penh attending. Over the course of the 2-day event, 60 exhibition booths were set up to accomodate the attendees, additionionally, 90 industry's leading speakers were invited to join the keynotes and panels that were organized, to share their experience and expertise on a variety of topics relating to digital technology. Participants had the opportunity to learn about coding, web development, and other digital technologies, as well as network with industry professionals. The event was well-received by attendees as seen with the level of participation and engagement from all over social media.
