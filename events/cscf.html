CADT Student Club Festival

15th August 2022

CADT Student Association recently organized a festival event called CADT Student Club Festival to showcase all the newly formed student clubs varying from hard-skill clubs and art clubs all the way to sport clubs. The event was open to all generations of CADT students and the general public. It was held on CADT campus ground.

The festival featured a variety of activities and entertainment. There were food and drink booths, competitions, and live music. The student clubs also had booths set up to showcase their work and activities. The event was a great opportunity for students to learn more about the clubs and to get involved in the school community. It was also a chance for the student association to organize competitons for the students to partake. We made sure to include a variety of competition, such as sprint, ping pong, basketball, football, and esports competitions. We put a lot of effort into making sure our competitions ran smoothly.


The event was a great way to bring the student body together and celebrate the newly formed clubs. It was a great opportunity for students to get involved and make new friends. CADT Student Association would like to thank everyone who attended the festival and helped to make it a success and enjoyable for everyone. We are looking forward to organizing more events like this in the future.
