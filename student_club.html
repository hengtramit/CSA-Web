HARD-SKILL CLUBS
Coding Club
A place or community where seniors and juniors gather to share their passion and knowledge in coding and find help when facing problems.
English Club
The primary goals of English club are communicating and practicing English. Its activities may incorporate reading, writing, listening and speaking.
Design Club
This club is a gathering point for students interested in all disciplines of Design with the aim of promoting an enthusiasm for visual arts.
Robotic Club
The purpose of Robotic Club encourages student to be creative in problem solving, critical thinking especially engineering.
ART CLUBS
Traditonal Dancing Club
The main purposes of the traditional dancing club are to represent the identity of Cambodia and a conservation of traditional wealth.
Cooking Club
Simply put, Cooking club is a group of students, that get together to take on a cooking project or explore an unfamiliar cuisine or cooking technique.
Music Club
The purpose of Music Club is to provide a musical practice space for students and coordinate student and non-student performances on and off-campus.
Media Club
Media Club engages students who exhibit interest and talent in photography, filming. Students are able to develop their skills through handling the media aspects of major school events.
SPORT CLUBS
Football Club
The main purposes of Football Club is to promote health, unity and interaction in sport. Students get to express themselves through student-formed tournament.
Basketball Club
The purpose of Basketball Club is to provide opportunities for student to engage in activity that involving sports and also to give them a stage to show off their basketball talent.
Table Tennis Club
Table Tennis Club is a community of student table tennis players which educate students in the rules and techniques, and create a space for matchmaking.
E-Sport Club
E-Sport Club helps students learn how to approach video games in a way that brings out the educational side of them.
